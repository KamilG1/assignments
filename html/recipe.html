Recipe: My Infamous Fried Potatoes

Required: Skillet or frying pan, cutting board, chefs knife, potatoe peeler, potatoes, oil, seasonings

Duration: 15-20 Minutes


Link to my Index page


What you will need:
1. Oil. Ideally oil that's more natural for great heart health, but any oil works just fine.
2. Potatoes. I prefer gold potatoes but any potatoes works just fine.
3. Seasonings. This is just what I use but you can use anything you prefer - Cumin, Lawrys, Paprika, Salt, Pepper, Onion powder, Garlic powder, Parsley flakes, Chilli powder, and Cayenne.
4. Cutting board, chefs knife, lid for skillet or pan.

What you will do:
1. Pour oil on skillet, heat up temps to 4-5 (less than half the nob) and let sit while you prep the potatoes.
2. Rinse potatoes (skin) and peel, rinse again (no skin) and set them on cutting board for prepping. Dice potatoes to small cubes then place into skillet.
3. Lightly mix potatoes in with the oil, after mixing with oil begin to add your seasonings and mix in well.
4. Place lid over potaotes and let cook, every 2-4 minutes give potatoes and light mix, after 10-15 minutes turn stove off and remove potatoes from pan.
5. You can add anything you want after, I myself like to add shredded cheese on top while it's still hot so it melts into the potatoes.
